Seb
Director + Co-founder
An award winning creative technologist and multidisciplinary innovator. Spanning products, architecture, interiors and exhibitions, his work seeks to inhabit, define and inspire an optimistic future. Seb has collaborated with award-winning design and architecture firms in London, Shanghai and Hong Kong, including Unknown Works, CHAP and Aedas.
Ori
Director + Co-founder
A passionate engineer, designer, and researcher fusing creativity and science towards tangible human impact. With a background in innovation strategy and electronic engineering, he has led visionary projects fueled by interdisciplinary research across digital and physical products. He has held diverse impactful roles at GSK, Setmixer, Dotplot, and Epicue. His work has resulted in scientific publications in electronics engineering journals and direct generation of intellectual property.
Pri
Director + Co-founder
Pri is an award-winning designer and strategist, dedicated to creating lasting and positive societal impact through technical and experiential innovation. With a background in innovation strategy and interaction design, he has consulted for Adobe and the World Health Organisation, delivering projects that range from communication campaigns to user experience research.
